Title: Азия Экспресс Money Transfer

Азия Экспресс offers specialized services for money transfers in partnership with Агробанк. Customers can receive money transfers through Агробанк without incurring any service charges. However, it is important to note that Азия Экспресс does not provide the option to send money transfers. For receiving money, ensure that the transfer is directed through Агробанк to take advantage of the free service.
